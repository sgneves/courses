0. name: The name of the visitor.
1. appt_made_date: The date and time that the appointment was created.
2. appt_start_date: The date and time that the appointment was scheduled to start.
3. appt_end_date: The date and time that the appointment was scheduled to end.
4. visitee_namelast: The last name of the visitee (the person the visitor was meeting with).
5. visitee_namefirst: The first name of the visitee.
6. meeting_room: The room in which the appointment was scheduled.
7. description: Optional comments added by the WAVES operator.
